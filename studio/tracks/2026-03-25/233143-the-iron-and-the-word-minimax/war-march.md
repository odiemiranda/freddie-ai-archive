Epic Celtic war ballad, 88 BPM, D minor key. Song information: military march anthem, outdoor battlefield atmosphere, primal and defiant, building from authoritative command to full army chorus, themes of forging weapons through the night and transformation through ordeal. Vocal information: lead male vocalist with commanding deep baritone, chest voice resonance projecting authority, measured military cadence on verses like addressing assembled soldiers, clear articulation over reverb. Male choir on choruses — disciplined unison chanting, tight timing, powerful and controlled not rowdy, voices locked in rhythmic precision like soldiers marching in step. Deep sustained group humming on intro and outro, solemn and ancient. Instrumental production: war drums prominent and driving, deep thunderous hits on beats one and three, military snare roll building into choruses. Bodhrán accenting the march rhythm, right of center. Performance by structure: intro is deep solemn humming establishing the weight, verses are commanding spoken delivery with drums and sparse guitar, pre-chorus layers group voices descending, chorus is full military choir with war drums at maximum power, bridge drops to isolated voice nearly whispering the confession of fear, final chorus is the loudest most powerful moment — every voice every drum.
