### Production Notes
- This is the "you're hearing this through a stone wall" version — everything has room and reverb
- Higher Weirdness (40%) gives Suno permission to add organic imperfections and room ambience
- Lower Style Influence (60%) lets the cavernous space breathe more loosely
- Intro/outro humming should echo and decay slowly — "Echoing" and "Long reverb tail" tags guide this
- War drums should feel distant and thunderous, not close and punchy
- The whole track should sound like a live recording in a stone tavern, not a studio
- "Voices bleeding into reverb tail" tells Suno to let the chanting decay naturally between sections
