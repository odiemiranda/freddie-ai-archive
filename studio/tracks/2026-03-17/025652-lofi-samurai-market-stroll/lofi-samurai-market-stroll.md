## Variation Summary

1. **Market Gate Swagger** — Entering the market. Shamisen carries a bold melodic line over dusty boom-bap drums and reggae bass. The confident walk through the crowd, sun overhead, afternoon energy.
2. **Cobblestone Browse** — Browsing the stalls. Celtic harp arpeggios drift over a lazy jazz groove with shamisen adding color. Unhurried curiosity, picking things up and putting them down, taking it all in.
3. **Weapon Shop Approach** — Locking onto the destination. Both instruments lock into a tight groove as the bass deepens and the purpose sharpens. The crowd parts, the shop sign is in view, you know exactly what you came for.

The three variations tell a mini story: arrive → browse → lock on. Each works standalone but they also flow as a sequence.

- Medieval market atmosphere comes from the acoustic texture of shamisen + harp, not from literal sound effects

- Keep bass prominent — it's the street underfoot, the steady forward motion
