## Production Notes

- Imagine Dragons live and die by dynamics: whisper-to-roar. Verses must stay restrained so choruses hit like a siege engine
- Bodhrán and frame drum are the key to replacing their signature powerful percussion — use war-drum patterns, not gentle folk rhythms
- Hurdy-gurdy drone effectively replaces their electronic textures and synth pads
- Dan Reynolds' raw, gravelly power is the target vocal — think battle cry, not ballad
- "Stomp-clap" rhythms (like Radioactive, Believer) translate well to bodhrán and hand-clap patterns
- Iterate 4-8 times — Suno handles anthemic Celtic well but may default to gentle folk; re-roll if it loses the power
