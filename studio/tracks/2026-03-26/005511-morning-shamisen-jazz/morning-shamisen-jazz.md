## Production Notes
- Shamisen phrasing must stay "soft, restrained" — NOT "muted" (dangerous vibe word) or "lazy" (→ aimless)
- Harp sits low and wide — support role, never competing with shamisen's register
- Brushed drums only — no kick-heavy patterns, no hi-hat prominence
- Vinyl crackle and tape warmth are texture, not production style — keep them subtle
- All variations end with [Fade Out] or [Sustained] for loop compatibility
- Dangerous vibes translated: lazy→laid-back, muted→soft/restrained, quiet→gentle
