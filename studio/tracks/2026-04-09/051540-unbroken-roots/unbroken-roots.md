> **Key choice — C Phrygian:** One whole step below the source's D Minor, with the Phrygian flat-2nd adding ritual/ancient darkness without pushing the vocal below Suno's articulation floor. Phrygian is the mode of Gregorian chant and doom metal — the actual mode of subterranean ritual. Tonic C keeps chorus chord tones (C–Eb–G) in the articulable zone; going below C risks sub-100Hz collapse.
