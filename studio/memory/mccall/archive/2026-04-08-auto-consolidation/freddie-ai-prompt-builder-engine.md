## Knowledge
The Freddie AI Prompt Builder Engine is a comprehensive tool designed for effective music-related prompt generation, incorporating advanced strategies and linguistic processing for high-quality, nuanced outputs.

**Key Features:**
*   **Structured Content Strategy**: Utilizes a 'MiniMax 6-section prose strategy' to structure content, ensuring logical flow and comprehensive coverage within generated prompts.
*   **Linguistic Accuracy**: Employs 'Phonetics syllable fallback' and 'LY6 homograph detection' to enhance linguistic precision, addressing nuances in pronunciation and word meaning crucial for music lyrics.

**Development Rigor:**
The engine's development involved a rigorous multi-phase process, including:
*   **Architecture & Design**: Phase 4 architecture for the MiniMax Style strategy was completed, involving council review (Gemini/Grok/MiniMax) and 8 logged decisions.
*   **Task Management**: Detailed task documents were created, leading to single-dispatch development cycles.
*   **Extensive Testing**: Both Phase 4 and Phase 5 components underwent extensive testing (23 tests for MiniMax strategy, 22 tests for phonetics integration).
*   **Code Review & Refinement**: Both phases completed code reviews, identifying and resolving multiple findings (e.g., 3 warnings for Phase 4, 6 findings for Phase 5) to ensure quality and architectural alignment.
*   **Comprehensive Completion**: The entire Prompt Builder Engine was successfully completed, encompassing all 9 subtasks, demonstrating a high degree of rigor in delivering sophisticated tooling.

## Why
The Prompt Builder Engine is crucial for generating high-quality, consistent, and linguistically accurate music prompts. Its structured content approach ensures coherence, while advanced linguistic features address the specific challenges of music lyrics. The rigorous development process guarantees a robust, well-tested, and reliable tool, minimizing errors and maximizing creative potential.
