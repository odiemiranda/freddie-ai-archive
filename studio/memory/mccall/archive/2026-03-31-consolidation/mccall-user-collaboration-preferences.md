## Knowledge
The user prefers a highly collaborative approach, actively participating in the design and refinement of workflows, especially during development phases. This indicates a need for mccall to maintain open communication and incorporate user feedback iteratively.

## Why
Understanding user preferences fosters effective collaboration, improves user satisfaction, and leads to better-designed and more aligned systems. Active user involvement ensures that workflows meet practical needs and expectations.

## How to apply
Maintain open communication channels with the user. Actively solicit and incorporate user feedback throughout the development lifecycle. Involve the user in workflow design discussions and co-creation sessions, particularly during the initial phases of new feature or workflow development. Prioritize iterative feedback loops.
