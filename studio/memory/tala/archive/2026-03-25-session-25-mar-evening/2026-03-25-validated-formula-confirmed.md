The full Distill → Confirm → Build workflow produced a clean, great-sounding track on first generation. No iterations needed.

## The validated formula

**Style block:**
```
[Instrumental] Jazz fusion, shamisen melodic phrases, harp low-register pad chords as background warmth, soft drums with laid-back swing, Dm7 to Gmaj7 to Em7 to A7, D Major, 88 BPM, tape warmth, rolled-off highs, relaxed, unhurried, loop-friendly, clean mix, separated instruments [No Vocals] [Background Music]
```

**Lyrics block:**
```
[Instrumental]

[Intro]
[Shamisen plays a smooth melodic phrase alone, unhurried]
[Soft space, room to breathe]

[Build]
[Drums fade in with a light swinging backbeat]
[Shamisen carries the melody, smooth and leading]
[Harp enters low register only, soft pad-like chords behind]

[Hook]
[Shamisen leads every phrase, flowing and warm]
[Harp stays in low register, gentle bed underneath]
[Drums hold an easy pocket]

[Hook]
[Shamisen picks up a new melodic line, still leading]
[Harp low chords underneath, no high notes, no melody]
[Groove stays even and consistent]

[Outro]
[Harp fades first]
[Shamisen alone over soft drums]
[Fade Out]

[No Vocals]
[End]
```

## What made it work

1. **Full workflow** — distill caught genre problem (groove pop → jazz fusion), vibe words, instrument labels
2. **Style = palette + behavior + dynamics** — not bare names, not full arrangement
3. **Harp subordinated** in both Style ("background warmth") and Lyrics ("low register only", "no high notes, no melody")
4. **Neutral label** "harp" not "celtic harp"
5. **"smooth melodic phrase"** not "plucks" for shamisen
6. **"laid-back swing"** not "lazy swing"
7. **"relaxed, unhurried"** not "subtle melodic movement, consistent dynamics"
8. **88 BPM** — slower gives two plucked instruments breathing room
9. **Jazz fusion** — clear rhythm template Suno understands
10. **18-item exclude list** — aggressive blocking of all competing instruments

## Key lesson

Never skip the Distill → Confirm cycle. The full workflow caught 3 critical issues (genre, instrument label, BPM) that every shortcut attempt missed.
