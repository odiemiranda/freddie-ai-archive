# Platform Mechanics & Safeguards

## 1. The Three Sliders
| Slider | Control | Sweet Spot |
|--------|---------|------------|
| **Style Influence** | Genre purity | 80-90% for specific techniques |
| **Weirdness** | Creative risk/deviation | 50% for most musical results; 10% for technical vocals |
| **Audio Influence** | Reference clip similarity | 40-60% for groove; 70%+ for persona consistency |

- **Audio Influence** only appears when a reference clip is uploaded. It captures "feel" and "groove," not exact notes.

## 2. Artist Name Collisions (Blocked Words)
Suno silently filters words that match certain artist names, replacing them with vague genre tags.
- **Critical Failure:** "Skank" (matches Brazilian band Skank).
- **Replacement:** Suno replaces "skank guitar" with "Reggae/Ska/Pop Rock," destroying the specific instrument direction.
- **Safe Alternatives:** "offbeat guitar chop", "reggae offbeat strum", "choppy offbeat guitar".
