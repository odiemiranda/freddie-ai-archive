## [Callback:] Tag Confirmed

NOT an Extend feature. It's musical memory within a single generation — tells Suno to reference/reuse musical material from a named section.

### Examples:
- `[Callback: Chorus melody]` — bring back the chorus melody in this section
- `[Callback: continue with same vibe as chorus]` — maintain the energy/feel
- `[Callback: Verse 1 groove]` — reference the groove from verse 1

### Use cases:
- Bridge that echoes the chorus melody over stripped instrumentation
- Final chorus that references the intro
- Outro that calls back verse 1's feel

### Tested:
Bridge with `[Callback: Chorus melody]` + `[Instrument: Stripped back | single guitar]` successfully brought back the chorus melody over a quieter bed.
