## Style Prompt Construction Tips

### Combining Genres
Use "meets" or "with [genre] influences":
- "Indie folk meets dream pop with ambient influences"
- "Trap R&B with neo-soul vocal approach"
- "City pop with modern synth pop production"

### Specifying Era
Add decade references for tonal flavor:
- "80s synth pop" — Analog, neon, drum machines
- "90s grunge" — Raw, distorted, flannel
- "2000s emo" — Emotional, guitar-driven, confessional
- "70s disco" — Funky, four-on-the-floor, strings

### Production Descriptors
Add production quality terms:
- "Polished, radio-ready" — Clean, compressed, bright
- "Raw, demo-quality" — Unpolished, intimate, real
- "Lo-fi, tape-saturated" — Warm, imperfect, vintage
- "Spacious, cinematic" — Wide, reverb-heavy, dramatic
- "Minimalist, stripped-back" — Few elements, lots of space
- "Maximalist, dense" — Many layers, wall of sound
