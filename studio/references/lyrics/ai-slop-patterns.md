# AI-Slop Patterns

Patterns that AI models default to when they lack specific imagery. These are advisory — the critic uses judgment per-instance. A phrase from this list that's grounded with concrete detail can pass. A phrase that floats abstractly should be flagged.

**For lo-fi clichés with earn-it rules, see `lyrics-structural.md` (lines 396-412).**
**For hard-banned words (soul, heart, fire, desire, darkness), see `lyrics-structural.md` (lines 15-18).**

## Universal AI-Slop

Phrases every AI model reaches for. Genre-independent. If you see these, the lyrics were probably generated on autopilot.

| Pattern | Why it's slop | Better direction |
|---------|--------------|-----------------|
| neon lights / neon glow / neon signs | Default "urban night" shorthand | Which sign? What does it advertise? What color bleeds onto the sidewalk? |
| echoes in the void | Sounds deep, says nothing | Echoes of what? Where? An echo in a parking garage is different from an echo in your head |
| shadows dance / dancing shadows | Generic "dark mood" filler | Shadows from what? Cast by what light? Moving because of what? |
| whispers in the dark | AI's favorite spooky phrase | Who's whispering? What are they saying? Why can't they speak up? |
| fragments of / pieces of / shards of | Abstract "broken" metaphor | Fragment of what specific thing? A broken plate looks different from a broken promise |
| fading memories / distant memories | Abstract nostalgia | Which memory? What's the one detail that makes it hurt? |
| lost in time / time stands still | Temporal vagueness | What stopped? The clock on the wall? Your breath? The song on the radio? |
| digital / pixels / screens | Default "modern life" | Which screen? What's on it? A notification? A photo? A cursor blinking? |
| electric / static / signals | Pseudo-tech imagery | Static from what? An old TV? A bad phone call? The space between stations? |
| infinite / eternity / forever | Scale without substance | Forever doing what? Infinity of what? |
| beneath the surface | Implies depth without providing it | Surface of what? Water, skin, a conversation? |
| weight of the world | Dead metaphor | Which specific weight? A backpack? A phone that won't stop buzzing? |
| shattered / shattered dreams | Overused "broken" | What broke? How did it sound? What does the debris look like? |
| drowning in | Overused intensity metaphor | Drowning in what specifically? Paperwork is different from silence |
| walls closing in | Claustrophobia cliché | Which walls? What room? Is it literal or the feeling before a panic attack? |

## Genre-Specific Clichés

### Pop
| Pattern | Better direction |
|---------|-----------------|
| dancing all night | What kind of dancing? Where? With whom? What song? |
| party all night | What makes THIS party different from every other? |
| living for the moment | Which moment? Make it specific |
| you light up my world | What specifically changed when they arrived? |
| can't stop this feeling | Name the feeling. Describe where you feel it in your body |
| on top of the world | Where are you actually standing? What can you see? |

### Rock
| Pattern | Better direction |
|---------|-----------------|
| breaking chains / breaking free | Free from what? Chains made of what? |
| rebel without a cause | What's the actual cause? Even rebels have reasons |
| burn it down / watch it burn | Burn what? A bridge? A letter? A relationship? |
| scream into the void | Where are you screaming? A car? A pillow? A rooftop? |
| fight the system | Which system? Be specific about what's broken |
| rise from the ashes | What burned? What's the first thing you rebuild? |

### Country
| Pattern | Better direction |
|---------|-----------------|
| trucks / pickup trucks | What's in the truck bed? What sticker's on the bumper? |
| beer / cold beer | What brand? What bar? Who's buying? |
| dirt roads | Which dirt road? Where does it go? What's at the end? |
| small town | Which town? What's the one thing everyone knows about it? |
| Friday night lights | What's the score? Who's winning? Who are you sitting with? |
| front porch | What's on the porch? A broken chair? A dog? What can you hear? |

### Indie / Singer-Songwriter
| Pattern | Better direction |
|---------|-----------------|
| coffee shop romance | Which coffee shop? What did they order? What were they reading? |
| autumn leaves | Which tree? What color? Where did they land? |
| vinyl records | Which record? Who put it on? Why that one right now? |
| rainy afternoons | What kind of rain? What are you doing besides watching it? |
| empty notebooks | What was supposed to be written? Why is it still blank? |
| faded photographs | Of what? Who's in it? Where was it taken? What's the crease? |

### Rap / Hip-Hop
| Pattern | Better direction |
|---------|-----------------|
| came from nothing | What specific "nothing"? Which block? What was dinner? |
| stacking paper | How much? Where's it going? What's the first thing you bought? |
| haters / they don't want to see me win | Who specifically? What did they say? |
| real ones know | Know what? Be specific about the shared knowledge |
| from the bottom | Bottom of what? Describe the starting point |
| never look back | What are you not looking back at? The apartment? The city? |

### R&B / Soul
| Pattern | Better direction |
|---------|-----------------|
| your body / your touch | Which part of the body? What does the touch feel like? |
| all night long | Doing what all night? Be specific |
| make you feel | Feel what? Describe the sensation, not the category |
| between the sheets | What's actually happening? A conversation? Silence? Sleep? |
| late night texts | What does the text say? What time? What's the reply? |

### Electronic / EDM
| Pattern | Better direction |
|---------|-----------------|
| feel the bass / feel the beat | Where in your body? Your chest? Your teeth? |
| let the music take control | What does losing control look like for you? |
| hands up / hands in the air | What are the hands reaching for? |
| lost in the sound | Which sound? The kick? The synth? Someone's voice? |
| energy / pure energy | Energy doing what? Moving where? |

### Jazz
| Pattern | Better direction |
|---------|-----------------|
| smoky room / smoky bar | What brand of smoke? Whose cigarette? What's the bartender doing? |
| midnight serenade | Where? Who's listening? What instrument? |
| blue notes | Which note? What makes it blue for you specifically? |
| improvisation of life | Life doing what? Name the moment that felt unscripted |

### Metal
| Pattern | Better direction |
|---------|-----------------|
| darkness / embrace the dark | Dark how? Empty-room dark? Buried-alive dark? |
| blood / rivers of blood | Whose blood? From where? After what? |
| throne / kingdom | Kingdom of what? A bedroom? A stage? An empty parking lot? |
| unleash the beast | What beast? What does it actually do when loose? |

### Folk
| Pattern | Better direction |
|---------|-----------------|
| rolling hills | Which hills? What's growing on them? What season? |
| old oak tree | How old? What's carved in it? Who sits under it? |
| wandering soul | Wandering where? On foot? Why can't they stop? |
| the river / down by the river | Which river? What color is it? What's floating in it? |

### Ambient / Lo-fi
| Pattern | Better direction |
|---------|-----------------|
| drifting away | Drifting from what? Toward what? |
| peaceful silence | What sounds are hiding in the "silence"? |
| ocean waves | Which ocean? What time? What washes up? |
| breathing deeply | Where? What do you smell? |

**Note:** Lo-fi has additional clichés with earn-it rules in `lyrics-structural.md` (rainy window, coffee shop, late night drive, city lights, fading memories, vinyl spinning, sunrise/sunset, empty room, lost in thought).

## Romantic Clichés (Cross-Genre)

These appear in every genre. Flag when they appear without a specific grounding detail.

| Pattern | Better direction |
|---------|-----------------|
| heart on my sleeve | What's the actual vulnerable thing you showed? |
| set me free | Free from what? What does freedom look like tomorrow morning? |
| meant to be | What made you think that? A moment? A coincidence? |
| falling for you | When did you first notice? What tipped you? |
| two hearts beat as one | What's the actual moment of sync? A shared laugh? A simultaneous sigh? |
| you complete me | What was missing before? What's different now? |
| love at first sight | What did you actually see first? Their hands? Their laugh? The way they stood? |
| can't live without you | What specific daily thing becomes impossible? |
| you're my everything | Name three things. That's more real than "everything" |

## Emotional Flatteners

Abstract emotion words that SAY instead of SHOW. These tell the listener how to feel instead of making them feel it.

| Flattener | Better direction |
|-----------|-----------------|
| lonely | What does the loneliness look like? An empty chair? A phone with no notifications? |
| broken | Broken how? A cracked voice? Hands that won't stop shaking? |
| hopeless | What's the thing you stopped hoping for? |
| empty inside | What used to fill that space? |
| lost | Lost where? In a city? In a conversation? In your own house? |
| scared | Of what specifically? The dark? The silence? The phone ringing? |
| angry | At what? How does it manifest? Clenched jaw? Broken glass? Silence? |
| sad | The weakest word in songwriting. Replace with the IMAGE that caused the sadness |
| happy | Same problem. What does the happiness look like from the outside? |
| hurt | Where does it hurt? Body or mind? What caused it? |
| free | Free to do what? Name the first thing |
