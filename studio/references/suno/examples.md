## The Three-Tier Build

**Tier 1 → Tier 2 → Tier 3**

Example: Love Ballad
1. "Slow R&B ballad"
2. "Slow R&B ballad, romantic and intimate, piano and strings"
3. "Slow R&B ballad, romantic and intimate, piano and strings, smooth male vocals, late-night atmosphere"

Example: Workout Music
1. "Fast electronic"
2. "Fast electronic, aggressive and driving, heavy bass and synths"
3. "Fast electronic, aggressive and driving, heavy bass and synths, no vocals, 145 BPM, beast-mode energy"

Example: Road Trip
1. "Upbeat rock"
2. "Upbeat rock, classic and driving, electric guitar and drums"
3. "Upbeat rock, classic and driving, electric guitar and drums, anthemic chorus feel, windows-down highway energy"
