# The Whisper

> Breathy soft tenor — ASMR intimacy, bedroom vulnerability, every word a secret

---

## Voice Profile

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Register** | Soft tenor |
| **Tone** | Breathy, tender, vulnerable, close-mic |
| **Delivery** | Barely above a whisper, intimate, ASMR-close |
| **Energy** | Minimal — never rises, never projects. Everything feels private |
| **Pacing** | Slow, gentle, with natural breath between phrases |

## Style Tags

```
soft tenor male vocals, breathy and intimate, ASMR-close delivery, barely above a whisper, vulnerable and tender, soft high-end, analog warmth
```

## Exclude Style

```
loud, belting, powerful vocals, rock, electronic, heavy drums, distortion, auto-tune, gang vocals, brass
```

## Lyrics Technique

### Vocal direction tags (use before specific lines)
```
[Breathy vocals, Intimate, Close-mic, Barely singing]    ← verses, main whisper delivery
[Soft tenor, Tender, Whispered]                           ← chorus, stays soft — not a lift
[Breathy vocals, Vulnerable, Even softer]                 ← second verse, deeper intimacy
[Whispered, Close-mic, Almost spoken]                     ← bridge, most vulnerable moment
[Humming, Breathy, Dissolving]                            ← outro, disappearing into silence
```

### Writing rules
- **Never raise the volume** — the chorus is NOT a lift. Same whisper level, just more emotionally exposed
- **Ellipses for breath and hesitation** — "I just... keep forgetting" — the pause is the vulnerability, not the words
- **Tiny domestic details** — open windows, sweaters on chairs, the smell of rain. The smallest things carry the heaviest weight
- **Lines under 7 syllables** — short lines prevent the voice from building momentum into singing
- **No rhyme** — or only accidental rhyme. Forced rhyme feels performative, this voice is private
- **Bridge as the quietest moment** — two lines max. Almost spoken. The least amount of words with the most weight
- **Humming for outros** — breathy, dissolving, like falling asleep mid-thought
- **Parenthetical silence** — leave emotional space. What ISN'T said matters more than what is

### Line examples
```
I left the window open
So the room would smell like rain
You always liked that... the cold part
I just liked that you'd stay
```

```
I know you're awake...
I can tell by how quiet you're being
```

## Best Paired With

| Genre | Why |
|-------|-----|
| Bedroom pop | Minimal production keeps focus on the breath |
| Lo-fi | Vinyl warmth adds intimacy without volume |
| Ambient | Sparse arrangement lets the whisper fill the space |
| Acoustic ballad | Soft guitar/piano supports without competing |

## Advanced Settings

| Setting | Value | Reason |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Weirdness | 20-30% | Voice must stay controlled and soft — no surprises |
| Style Influence | 70-80% | High influence locks the breathy delivery, prevents drift into full singing |

## Tested

- 2026-03-21: Bedroom pop, E Minor, 68 BPM — breathy whisper delivery held throughout. Chorus stayed at whisper level without lifting. Bridge at two lines landed as most vulnerable moment. High Style Influence (75%) was key to preventing Suno from drifting into full singing.
