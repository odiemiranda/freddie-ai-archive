# The Showman

> Theatrical rock tenor — over-the-top delivery, half-singing half-shouting, stadium energy in a small room

---

## Voice Profile

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Register** | Mid-range rock tenor |
| **Tone** | Theatrical, playful, crunchy, explosive |
| **Delivery** | Half-singing half-shouting, dramatic, slightly unhinged |
| **Energy** | Maximum — performing like the room is a stadium and the audience needs converting |
| **Pacing** | Punchy verses, dramatic pauses between shouts, building to explosive choruses |

## Lyrics Technique

### Vocal direction tags (use before specific lines)
```
[Spoken word, Theatrical, Building excitement]              ← intro, hyping the room
[Rock tenor, Playful, Theatrical, Slightly unhinged]        ← verses, hamming it up
[Building, Dramatic, Getting louder]                        ← pre-chorus, escalation
[Powerful vocals, Explosive, Theatrical shouting]            ← chorus, full blast
[Spoken word, Dramatic pause, Theatrical whisper]            ← bridge, the quiet before the storm
[Screaming vocals, Full band, Explosive, Everything cranked] ← final chorus, peak
[Spoken word, Breathless, Laughing]                          ← outro, coming down from the high
```

### Writing rules
- **Exclamation-marked pauses** — "NOT! A! MISTAKE!" and "THREE! MORE! TIMES!" — forces Suno to punch each word separately. This is the Showman's signature move
- **Half-singing half-shouting** — lines alternate between melodic phrases and shouted declarations. Never fully one or the other
- **Theatrical spoken intros** — "Alright, alright, ALRIGHT!" — the Showman always addresses the audience before the music starts
- **Humor through escalation** — "landlord said one more time / so I gave him THREE MORE TIMES" — comedy comes from doubling down, not backing down
- **Bridge as dramatic whisper** — drop to theatrical spoken word for 3-4 lines. The quiet moment that makes the final chorus explosion devastating
- **Guitar solo earns a section** — the Showman DEMANDS a guitar solo. Give it its own section with `[Guitar solo]` tags
- **ALL CAPS escalates across the song** — verse: normal case. Chorus: hook words in caps. Final chorus: everything caps
- **Breathless outro** — spoken word, laughing, out of breath. The performance just happened and the Showman is riding the high
- **Short shouted lines** — 4-7 syllables on chorus hooks. Longer lines get punched into segments with exclamation marks

### Line examples
