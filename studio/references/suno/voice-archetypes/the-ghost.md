# The Ghost

> Ethereal high tenor/falsetto — otherworldly, reverb-drenched, more texture than words

---

## Voice Profile

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Register** | High tenor, falsetto |
| **Tone** | Ethereal, haunting, reverb-drenched, distant |
| **Delivery** | Floating, otherworldly, fading in and out |
| **Energy** | Minimal presence — the voice exists at the edge of hearing |
| **Pacing** | Slow, drifting, with long pauses and ellipses between phrases |

## Lyrics Technique

### Vocal direction tags (use before specific lines)
```
[Ethereal vocals, Distant, Heavy reverb, Falsetto]       ← verses, floating above the mix
[Floating vocals, Otherworldly, Layered harmonies]        ← chorus, layered and haunting
[Ethereal vocals, Fading in and out, Reverb-heavy]        ← second verse, less present
[Whispered falsetto, Barely there, Maximum reverb]         ← bridge, almost gone
[Ethereal vocals, Full wash, Dissolving into reverb]       ← final chorus, disappearing
[Vocalizing, Ethereal, Disappearing]                       ← outro, wordless fade
```

### Writing rules
- **Voice as texture** — the Ghost is an instrument, not a narrator. Words matter less than the sound of words
- **Ellipses for dissolution** — "I was here... before the walls went up" — the voice drifts between phrases
- **Final chorus dissolves** — add ellipses to every line: "I'm the light... that moves..." — the voice becomes reverb
- **Short mysterious lines** — 5-7 syllables. Brief appearances, not full statements
- **No concrete storytelling** — the Ghost deals in impressions: moved curtains, lights in empty rooms, echoes. Suggestions, not facts
- **Bridge at maximum absence** — two lines of whispered falsetto. The ghost barely existing
- **Vocalizing for outros** — "Ooh... ooh... ooh..." — wordless, floating, disappearing
- **Layered harmonies on chorus** — Suno renders this as stacked vocal textures, reinforcing the otherworldly feel

### Line examples
```
I was here... before the walls went up
I was here... before they named the street
You walked through me once
And didn't feel a thing
```

```
Can you hear me...
Or just the echo...
```

## Best Paired With

| Genre | Why |
|-------|-----|
| Dream pop / shoegaze | Shimmering guitars and wash cymbals are the natural habitat |
| Ambient | Sparse arrangement lets the voice float as texture |
| Dark ambient | Haunting tone works with tension and unease |
| Post-rock | Instrumental builds with the voice as an atmospheric layer |

## Advanced Settings

| Setting | Value | Reason |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Weirdness | 55-65% | High weirdness lets Suno find the otherworldly space — the Ghost needs to feel uncanny |
| Style Influence | 45-55% | Moderate — too high locks it into a clean vocal, too low loses the ethereal quality |

## Tested

- 2026-03-21: Dream pop shoegaze, Ab Major, 74 BPM — ethereal falsetto confirmed floating above the mix. Final chorus with ellipses on every line dissolved into reverb naturally. Bridge at two whispered falsetto lines was the ghost barely existing. High Weirdness (60%) was key to the otherworldly feel.
