### Celtic New Age

Celtic New Age fuses Celtic themes, instruments, and melodies with New Age's lush, relaxation-forward production - airy synth pads, big reverb, gentle rhythms, and nature-leaning ambience. It crossed into the mainstream in the late 1980s via Enya/Clannad and stayed popular through the 1990s (e.g., Loreena McKennitt).
