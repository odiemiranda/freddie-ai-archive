Salsa is a New York-forged Afro-Caribbean dance music built on Cuban son/mambo roots and Puerto Rican influence, organized around clave, piano montuno, and bass tumbao, with congas, timbales, bongos/cowbell, and horn riﬀs. It developed in NYC in the 1940s-50s, was labeled "salsa" in the 1960s, and peaked in the 1970s.

### Tango

Tango is an Afro-European dance born in Buenos Aires and Montevideo in the late 19th century. Built on phrased melodies over 2/4 or 4/4 and driven by the bandoneón, it's played by an orquesta típica.
