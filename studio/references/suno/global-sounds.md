## Regional Music Styles & Cultural Fusion

> **Advanced Settings guidance:** Regional/cultural styles generally benefit from Weirdness 25-45% and Style Influence 65-85% — authenticity matters. For fusion experiments, raise Weirdness to 50-70% and lower Style Influence to 40-60%.

---

## BRAZIL 🇧🇷

### Bossa Nova
**Characteristics:** Nylon string guitar, gentle percussion, Portuguese feel, beach sunset
**BPM:** 70-90 | **Keys:** G Major, C Major, F Major

```
[Instrumental] Bossa nova, nylon string guitar, gentle percussion, warm bass, laid-back groove, G Major, 75 BPM, beach sunset atmosphere, João Gilberto influence [No Vocals]
```

### Samba
**Characteristics:** Brazilian percussion (surdo, tamborim), carnival energy, polyrhythmic
**BPM:** 95-120 | **Keys:** D Major, G Major

```
[Instrumental] Samba, Brazilian percussion, surdo and tamborim, carnival energy, D Major, 110 BPM, Rio de Janeiro street party atmosphere, bright and joyful [No Vocals]
```

### Baile Funk
**Characteristics:** Heavy bass, Miami bass influence, Rio favela energy
**BPM:** 130-145 | **Keys:** A Minor, D Minor

```
[Instrumental] Brazilian baile funk, heavy 808 bass, Rio favela energy, dance percussion, A Minor, 135 BPM, high energy party [No Vocals]
```

### MPB (Música Popular Brasileira)
**Characteristics:** Sophisticated arrangements, jazz-influenced, poetic
**BPM:** 80-110 | **Keys:** G Major, C Major, A Minor

```
[Instrumental] MPB style, sophisticated Brazilian arrangement, nylon guitar, subtle percussion, G Major, 95 BPM, Caetano Veloso influence, poetic and warm [No Vocals]
```
