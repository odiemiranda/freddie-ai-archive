# Relaxation Niche Prompts

Battle-tested prompts for meditation, relaxing piano, and spa/wellness music.

> **Note:** Some example prompts below use an older format with `[Intro]`/`[Build]` tags inline. The current best practice is: **Style block = sonic palette only** (genre, instruments, mood, BPM, key, texture). Structure tags go in the **Lyrics block**. Adapt these examples accordingly.

---

## MEDITATION MUSIC

### Mindfulness & Breathing

**Core Characteristics:**
- BPM: 55-70 (matches relaxed breathing)
- Keys: C Major, F Major, G Major
- Elements: Sparse tones, gentle bells, soft drones, long silences
- Atmosphere: Spacious, present, observational, non-intrusive
- Advanced Settings: Weirdness 40-60%, Style Influence 50-70% — some unpredictability keeps meditation engaging. Vary: 40/65, 50/55, 60/45

**Tagged Prompts:**

```
[Instrumental] Guided meditation background, soft drones, gentle bells, slow and spacious, spiritual mood, C Major, 60 BPM, mindfulness support [No Vocals] [Ambient]
```

```
[Instrumental] Zen meditation, sparse piano notes, long silences, soft bowl sounds, empty and peaceful, F Major, 55 BPM, present awareness [No Vocals] [Sustained]
```

```
[Instrumental] Walking meditation music, slow steady beat, nature sounds layered, present and grounded, G Major, 65 BPM, mindful movement [No Vocals]
```

```
[Instrumental] Yoga nidra music, very slow ambient, hypnotic tones, body scan energy, deeply restful, C Major, 50 BPM, sleep-edge meditation [No Vocals] [Sustained]
```

```
[Instrumental] Loving kindness meditation, warm tones, gentle piano, compassionate and open-hearted mood, F Major, 60 BPM, metta practice [No Vocals] [Ambient]
```

**Natural-Language Prompts:**

## RELAXING PIANO

### Solo Piano & Cinematic

**Core Characteristics:**
- BPM: 60-85 (gentle, unhurried)
- Keys: C Major, F Major, A Minor, D Minor
- Elements: Solo piano, minimal accompaniment, emotional dynamics
- Atmosphere: Intimate, tender, emotional, composed
- Advanced Settings: Weirdness 30-50%, Style Influence 60-80% — piano pieces need tighter style control for emotional coherence. Vary: 30/80, 40/70, 50/60

**Tagged Prompts:**

```
[Instrumental] Solo piano, slow tempo, gentle touch, simple melody, introspective and tender mood, A Minor, 70 BPM, intimate recital atmosphere [No Vocals]
```

```
[Instrumental] Cinematic piano, emotional build, soft dynamics, orchestral undertones, deeply moving, D Minor, 75 BPM, film score elegance [No Vocals]
```

```
[Instrumental] Rainy day piano, slow minor chords, melancholic but beautiful, nostalgic atmosphere, A Minor, 65 BPM, window-seat reflection [No Vocals]

[Instrumental] Healing piano, gentle touch, tender melody, emotional release, comforting and warm, F Major, 72 BPM, therapeutic calm [No Vocals]

## SPA & WELLNESS MUSIC

### Spa, Massage & Wellness

**Core Characteristics:**
- BPM: 50-70 (ultra-slow, non-intrusive)
- Keys: F Major, C Major, G Major (warm, safe)
- Elements: Soft flute, water sounds, gentle strings, nature layers
- Atmosphere: Serene, restorative, elegant, holistic
- Advanced Settings: Weirdness 25-45%, Style Influence 65-85% — spa music should be predictable and non-intrusive. Vary: 25/85, 35/75, 45/65

**Tagged Prompts:**

```
[Instrumental] Luxury spa music, soft flute, gentle water sounds, light strings, serene and elegant, F Major, 60 BPM, wellness retreat atmosphere [No Vocals] [Ambient]

[Instrumental] Massage therapy music, slow ambient, no percussion, warm and soothing, deeply relaxing, C Major, 55 BPM, therapeutic calm [No Vocals] [Sustained]
