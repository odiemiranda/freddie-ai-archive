# Still Warm

> Part of: Coffee Half-Full

---

### Approach
Jazzhop conversational. Four-line verses with internal slant rhyme and swung phrasing — someone thinking out loud over a dusty loop. Density stays low-to-moderate but every line lands on the pocket. Rhodes chords breathe between bars like a Philanthrope beat tape. The volta lives in the bridge: rushing flips to stillness.

### Style Block Notes
- Rhodes piano over a dusty, looped drum pattern — swung, not quantized
- Finger snaps and vinyl texture from the intro; brushed snare enters at chorus
- Warm lo-pass filtering on everything — close-mic, tape-saturated feel
- Bass should be warm and round, sitting under the Rhodes, not competing with vocal
- Bridge strips to Rhodes and voice only — the beat drops out completely
- Think Philanthrope, Aso, Funky DL — jazzhop beat tape energy, not smooth jazz
- Style should NOT say "coffee" or "morning" — lyrics carry theme, Style carries sound
