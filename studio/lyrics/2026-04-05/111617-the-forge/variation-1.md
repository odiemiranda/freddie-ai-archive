# V1: Narrative Cinematographer

## Approach
Scene-by-scene storytelling. Each line is a camera shot — wide tavern to close-up hands to memory of the anvil. The bridge is the volta: the call to The Forge shifts from duty to identity.

## Lyrics

```
[Short Instrumental Intro]
[Energy: Low]
[Mood: Ancient, Weary]
[Instrument: War drums distant pulse | Cello drone]
[Texture: Echoing stone hall]

[Verse 1]
[Energy: Low]
[Mood: Melancholy, Stoic]
[Vocal Style: Deep baritone, Gravelly, Spoken word]

The fire's down to coals, the ale's gone warm
```
