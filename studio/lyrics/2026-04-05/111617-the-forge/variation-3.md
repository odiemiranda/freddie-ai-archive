# V3: Structural Architect

## Approach
Non-linear timeline. Opens at the Forge (destination), drops to the tavern (present), bridges through the journey's cost, then reveals the road (past) AFTER the resolve. The listener realizes the story was told backwards. Callback phrase "The forge already knows my name" planted as longing, returns as declaration, closes as fate.
