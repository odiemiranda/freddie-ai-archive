# Tara Na Pre

> Part of: Galingan Natin

---

### Approach
**Conversational / Barkada** -- friends talking to each other. Casual Taglish code-switching, dialogue moments, call-and-response between voices. Feels like overhearing a group of friends hyping each other up on a Friday night. The volta in the bridge is one friend getting real for a moment before the others pull them back into the chorus.

### Lyrics
```

[Spoken word, casual]
Pre, gising ka na ba?

[Verse 1]
Sabi mo, "Wala akong pera"

Tsinelas mo sira, tawa lang tayo

Monday kayod, Friday laban
```
