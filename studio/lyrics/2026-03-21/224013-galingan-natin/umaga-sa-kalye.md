# Umaga sa Kalye

> Part of: Galingan Natin

---

### Approach
**Narrative** -- a day in the life, morning to night. Each verse advances the clock: waking up broke, grinding through the day, evening hangout with the barkada. Concrete details (barya, tsinelas, jeepney) anchor every section. The volta lands in the bridge: a quiet admission that they don't have much -- but the final chorus reframes "galingan natin" from hustle into something bigger than money.
