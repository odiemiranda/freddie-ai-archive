# Roll Call ng Sarap

> Part of: Ngayon naman, Mga Kuys

---

### Approach
Repetition-heavy/Chant -- chorus-forward, dish names used as rhythmic roll calls. Gang-shout friendly with dish names becoming percussive chants. Reggae-leaning density (4 lines/verse, 5-6 syl/line) with the chorus doing the heavy lifting. The verses are setup; the real energy is in the repeated hooks and dish-name chants.

### Style Block Notes
- This variation is built for gang shouts -- the Style's "stacked harmonies on the hook" should go hard on every chorus
- The (Sarap! Sarap! Sarap!) tag after each chorus is parenthesized intentionally -- Suno will sing it as a backing vocal chant, like a crowd echo
- Verses are sparse on purpose -- let the skank guitar and bass groove carry the space between dish-name drops
- The bridge volta compares sinigang broth to a mother's embrace, then pivots to "you just need the right company" -- the [Building] tag should ramp the full band back in
- Cowbell hits should be most prominent here since the rhythmic roll-call structure gives it natural landing points on turnarounds
